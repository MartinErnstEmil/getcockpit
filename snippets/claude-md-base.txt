---
title: Ask before non-trivial work
description: Clarify before starting anything beyond a typo fix
target: claude_md
section: workflows
priority: 20
tags: planning, ai-behavior
---
- Ask clarifying questions BEFORE starting non-trivial work
- For trivial work (typos, obvious fixes), proceed directly without asking
- Propose a brief plan (3-7 bullets) before complex implementations; await approval
